About Us

Stylist Retailers is a leading nationwide clothing and lifestyle brand, operating 300 stores across North America. We are dedicated to empowering individuals to express their unique style through fresh, modern fashion. Committed to enhancing your shopping experience, we're embracing innovative digital solutions to ensure seamless, personalized, and convenient interactions, both in-store and online.

Our Network

With 300 vibrant stores, Stylist Retailers boasts a significant presence across North America. From bustling urban centers like New York, Los Angeles, and Miami to key regional markets in the Midwest and Pacific Northwest, our physical locations offer a personalized shopping experience, including our highly sought-after Personal Shopper service. Beyond our brick-and-mortar footprint, our digital storefront provides 24/7 access to our latest collections. Our comprehensive e-commerce platform, ensures that our fresh, modern styles are accessible to every customer, everywhere, at any time.

Current Communication Systems

Stylist Retailers currently relies on a foundational set of communication tools to manage its operations and customer interactions:

  • Telephony: Cisco BroadWorks for internal and external voice communications.
  • Contact Center: An outsourced legacy call center solution, featuring basic IVR capabilities but lacking modern AI functionalities.
  • Channels: Voice & email only — no AI, chat, or self-service

Modernization Vision

Our vision is to elevate your experience by unifying our communication systems. This will deliver seamless digital interactions, quicker resolutions, and consistent, personalized service across all touchpoints. We're leveraging advanced tools to make every interaction effortless and tailored to your unique needs, ensuring a superior journey with us.

What We’re Solving For

  • Delivering seamless, consistent, and personalized interactions across all touchpoints
  • Ensuring every interaction, whether online or in-store, is smooth, consistent, and tailored to your individual preferences
  • Offering convenient, modern channels like chat, SMS, and self-service options so you can connect with us quickly and easily, on your terms
  • Getting you the right answers and solutions faster, preventing misrouted calls and ensuring our team has all the context needed to assist you efficiently
