About Us

Cascadia Health Network serves communities across Oregon and Washington through two acute-care hospitals and sixty outpatient clinics. Our mission is to deliver seamless, patient-centered care — whether in the hospital, at a neighborhood clinic, or through virtual services. As patient expectations evolve, we are modernizing the way our teams communicate so care teams can coordinate faster, and patients can reach us more easily.

Our Network

  • Hospitals: Cascadia Regional Medical Center (Portland, OR) & ValleyCare Hospital (Salem, OR)
  • Clinics: 60 locations (Primary Care, Pediatrics, Urgent Care, Specialty)
  • Staffing: 5,600+ employees · 450 contact center agents
  • Patient Interactions: 1M+ annually

Current Communication Systems

  • Hospitals: Legacy Nortel PBXs (voice only)
  • Clinics: Multiple CUCM + UCCX clusters (separately managed)
  • Channels: Voice & email only — no AI, chat, or self-service
  • Challenge: Disconnected systems slow transfers and coordination between sites
During surge events or outages, manual workarounds create delays for both staff and patients.

Modernization Vision

  • Hospitals: Migrate to CUCM on-prem integrated with Webex Calling + Webex Contact Center (hybrid continuity)
  • Clinics: Transition fully to Webex Cloud (Calling + Contact Center)
  • Goal: A resilient, unified platform that stays online during events — from earthquakes to pandemic surges

What We’re Solving For

  • Streamlined appointment scheduling and reminders
  • Faster nurse triage handoffs with shared context
  • Reduced repeat calls for prescription renewals
  • Continuity during outages and disaster events
  • Unified reporting across clinics and hospitals
  • Less manual work; more time for patient care

About Us

Welcome to Higher Education University, a vibrant and dynamic institution dedicated to fostering academic excellence, groundbreaking research, and a thriving student community. As a leading private university serving over 35,000 students and supported by 8,000 dedicated staff, we are committed to providing an unparalleled educational experience that prepares our graduates to lead and innovate in a rapidly changing world.
At Higher Education University, we believe that student success is paramount. That's why we are continuously evolving our approach to support services, embracing Intelligent Student Services to meet the diverse needs of our campus community. Through advanced digital platforms and the strategic integration of artificial intelligence, we are dedicated to Enhancing Experience by offering seamless, personalized, and accessible support across all facets of student life – from admissions and financial aid to academic advising and IT assistance.
Our mission extends beyond the classroom; we strive to Empower Success for every student, ensuring they have the resources and guidance needed to excel academically, grow personally, and achieve their career aspirations. We are proud of our rich history, our innovative spirit, and our unwavering commitment to creating an inclusive and supportive environment where every student can thrive. Join us in shaping the future.

Current Communication Systems

  • Campus: Cisco Webex Contact Center
  • Departments: Student Services, Admissions, Financial Aid, IT HelpDesk
  • Channels: Voice, email & chat. Limited self-service adoption and no AI
  • Challenge: Inefficient handling of student inquiries and services, particularly during peak times.
During peak times students suffer a poor experience, due to long wait times, abandoned calls, and repetitive tasks for staff.

Modernization Vision

The university's modernization vision for their service centers around optimizing their existing Webex Contact Center (WxCC) investment by fully leveraging AI capabilities to enhance efficiency, scalability, and the overall student digital experience.

  • Moving beyond a "lift-and-shift" from their old UCCX system to actively utilize advanced features.
  • Automate repetitive tasks, deflect common inquiries, and provide intelligent assistance.
  • Offering 24/7 self-service options and a more consistent, seamless experience across all service areas.
  • Handling seasonal spikes (registration, financial aid deadlines) without needing to add more staff

What We’re Solving For

  • Alleviating the burden on Student Services and Financial Aid staff, who are overwhelmed during peak periods
  • Reduce the high volume of repetitive FAQ calls
  • Introduce automation for simple tasks
  • Effectively handle seasonal spikes (registratioin, aid deadlines...)
  • Deliver a more consistent, seamless experience across all service areas
  • Drive continous improvement through better data and insights
